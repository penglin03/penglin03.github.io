Peng Lin

About

I obtained my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Washington State University, under the supervision of Dr. Yinghui Wu and Dr. Lawrence Holder. My research interests span the topics of graph data analytics, mining, and management. Specifically, I have been working on various graph querying and refinement problems for knowledge graphs, e.g., link prediction, graph data quality, etc, using either constraint-based or learning-based methods.
